Mines Game
Online Gambling Mechanics Demonstration

This demonstration shows how easily online gambling games can be manipulated. It is not a real gambling game.

How This Demonstrates Manipulation:

  • The "Toggle Rigging" button simulates how game operators can easily change win probabilities.
  • When rigging is on, the game becomes much harder to win, demonstrating how odds can be manipulated.
  • This shows why online gambling can be dangerous and unpredictable.
